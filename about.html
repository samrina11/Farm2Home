About Farm2Home

Connecting local farmers directly with customers for fresher produce, fairer prices, and stronger communities.

Our Mission

To create a simple, trusted marketplace that empowers small farmers by giving them direct access to customers, helping families eat healthier, and reducing food miles.

Our Vision

A sustainable local food network where farmers thrive, waste is minimized, and communities have easy access to fresh, seasonal produce.

What We Offer

  • Direct sales channel for farmers — fewer middlemen, fairer pay.
  • Fresh, seasonal produce delivered to your doorstep.
  • Transparent pricing, safe payments, and clear tracking for orders.

FAQ

How do farmers get paid?

Payments are transferred to the farmer's bank account or mobile wallet. We also support cash pickup at collection centers where available.

What if I don't have internet?

You can use SMS/USSD or contact a local agent who will help submit listings on your behalf.

Are there fees to join?

Basic listing is free. Premium features (featured listing, marketing packages) are paid — we offer fair, transparent pricing for all tiers.
